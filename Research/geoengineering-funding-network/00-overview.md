# Geoengineering Funding Network Research

## What We Know

A small network of ~11 billionaires and their foundations dominate global geoengineering funding. The money flows from tech and hedge fund wealth through nonprofit research programs, advocacy organizations, and increasingly into for-profit startups. The same individuals appear at multiple levels -- funding the research, shaping the policy, and investing in the companies that would profit from deployment.

## What We Believe

The pattern is not conspiracy but **convergent interest**: a small class of tech/finance elites who share an ecomodernist worldview, believe technology can solve climate change without systemic economic change, and stand to profit if geoengineering becomes normalized. The science has not kept pace with the commercial activity.

## What's Open

- Full funding amounts (much is undisclosed)
- Extent of fossil fuel industry involvement (stated exclusion, but indirect channels possible)
- Whether the for-profit companies have viable technology or just investor hype
- How government research agendas are shaped by philanthropic lobbying
- International dimensions (China, UAE, Saudi Arabia programs)
