# SkyLedger Coalition Strategy

We're the dashboard. They're the archive and the legal arm. Citizens land on SkyLedger, track flights, see who's behind it — and when they want to go deeper (patent evidence, lab results, legal action), we send them to the right ally. Clean, not overwhelming.

**Principle:** Stay in our lane, cross-link for everything else. Don't replicate — aggregate and connect.

## Ally Organizations

### Geoengineering Watch — The Goldmine
- **Contact:** admin@geoengineeringwatch.org
- **Lead:** Dane Wigington
- **Role:** The archive — 20+ years of documents, lab tests, documentary
- **Outreach tone:** "We appreciate what you're doing, we're available as a resource, feel free to use our flight tracking platform"
- **Key assets:**
  - **600+ weather modification patents** — every one numbered, dated, titled. From "Method of Producing Rain-Fall" (1891) to "System for Moderating Energy Absorption at Earth's Surface" (2023).
  - **Government document library** — 1978 Congressional report (~750 pages), "Weather as a Force Multiplier: Owning the Weather in 2025" (DOD), CIA weather warfare analysis, 1966 NASA weather mod programs, WMO statements, Senate hearings
  - **Aerial nanoparticle sampling data** — flew aircraft through contrail layers in 2019, SEM/TEM analysis showing aluminum and barium sulfate nanoparticles below 70 nanometers
  - **NWS/NOAA gag order story** — 2015, Public Employees for Environmental Responsibility asked Special Counsel to reverse illegal gag orders
  - **Health impact research** — aluminum/Alzheimer's connection, bumblebee studies (pathological aluminum levels), heavy metal environmental degradation
  - **Engineered weather event categories** — droughts, wildfires, winter storms, hurricanes
  - **"The Dimming"** documentary (25M+ views), testified as expert witness for Wyoming geoengineering ban
- **What they DON'T have (our gap):** No real-time flight tracking, no FAA owner lookup, no operator funding profiles, no transparency layer, no legislation tracker, no modern web platform

### Stop Geoengineering MN
- **Contact:** info@stopgeoengineeringmn.org
- **Role:** State-level legal action (sued Air Force in federal court for weather mod info)
- **Outreach tone:** Same as above — we're a tool, happy to help

### The GeoFight — What They Need From Us
- **Website:** thegeofight.com
- **Role:** Federal lawsuit coalition (Nicole Pearson, Blake Horwitz, Tom Renz)
- **Structure:** 501(c)(3), fiscal sponsor Greater Chicago Affordable Legal Services
- **Their biggest needs (exactly what we provide):**
  - **Court-admissible evidence** — our flight tracking with FAA registry data is public record
  - **Whistleblower portal** — they have one, we could cross-link
  - **Case evaluation intake** — citizens submit evidence of personal/community harm
- **Focus contaminants:** SO2, aluminum, barium, strontium

### ICAN (Informed Consent Action Network)
- **Website:** icandecide.org
- **Role:** FOIA bulldozer — forced disclosure of NSF "holy grail" SAI grant ($400K to Cornell/Indiana) and NOAA SABRE program

### Carnicom Institute
- **Website:** carnicominstitute.org
- **Role:** Independent environmental/biological research on aerosol samples
