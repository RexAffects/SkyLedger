# OpenSkies Outreach List

People and organizations to contact when the project is ready for public launch.

## Government / Political

- **EPA Administrator Lee Zeldin** — Stated publicly: "Americans have urgent questions about geoengineering. That era [of dismissing questions] is over." Ordered EPA to compile and publish everything they know about contrails and geoengineering.
- **Rep. Marjorie Taylor Greene** — Introduced the Clear Skies Act (H.R. 4403), chaired the "Playing God with the Weather" hearing. Recently left Congress but may still champion the cause.
- **Rep. Tim Burchett (TN)** — Active in the hearing, asked pointed questions about silver iodide safety. Tennessee was the first state to ban.
- **Rep. Anna Paulina Luna (FL)** — Florida's ban is the strongest (felony). Worth reaching out.

## Legal Organizations

- **The GeoFight** (thegeofight.com) — Nicole Pearson, Blake Horwitz, Tom Renz. Building federal lawsuit. Would benefit from OpenSkies as an evidence tool.
- **ICAN** (icandecide.org) — Filing FOIAs, uncovering funded SAI research.
- **Dane Wigington / Geoengineering Watch** — Testified as expert witness for state bills. Large audience.

## Social Media / Influencers

- Geoengineering Instagram accounts (Rex to identify specific ones)
- Weather modification awareness accounts on X/Twitter
- Independent journalists covering the topic

## Witnesses from the Hearing

- **Dr. Roger Pielke Jr.** — American Enterprise Institute. 500+ scientists signed his non-use agreement. Academic credibility.
- **Christopher Marks** — Meteorologist, CFACT. Clearly explained the science. Accessible voice.

## State-Level Contacts

- **Louisiana DEQ** — Running the Act No. 95 reporting portal. Could integrate with OpenSkies.
- **Florida complaint system administrators** — SB 56 created a public complaint system.
- **Texas Weather Modification Advisory Board** — Most transparent state program.

---

## Notes

- OpenSkies is a tool, not an advocacy org. Position it as: "We built this free tool for citizen transparency. Want to share it with your audience?"
- Lead with the flight tracker — it's the most impressive demo
- The congressional hearing page is the best "credibility link" to share with skeptics
